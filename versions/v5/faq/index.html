What kind of websites can I audit?

Any publicly accessible website. Landing pages, blogs, e-commerce stores, SaaS products, tools, portfolios — all work. Just enter the URL and our engine scans it across 24+ checkpoints.

What does "self-evolving" mean?

Our detection engine automatically updates its rules every week. We monitor Google Search Central, web.dev, and official best practice sources — when Google announces a new policy or recommendation, our engine learns it before most SEO tools catch up. No manual updates required.

How is this different from PageSpeed Insights?

PageSpeed Insights only checks page speed. We check 24+ factors including meta tags, headings, structured data, image alt attributes, internal/external links, content quality, OG/social tags, mobile responsiveness, HTTPS setup, site structure, and spam policy compliance. We also generate actionable fix code you can implement directly.

What SEO issues does the tool detect?

Our 24-point detection matrix covers: Titles & Meta (missing/duplicate/malformed), Headings (H1/H2 structure), Links (broken, ratio analysis), Image Alt (missing alt attributes), Structured Data (JSON-LD validation), Mobile (viewport, tap targets), OG Tags (social sharing), Content Quality (thin content, keyword density), SEO Foundation (robots, sitemap, canonical), HTTPS (SSL, mixed content), Site Structure (breadcrumbs, URL structure), and Spam (cloaking, thin affiliate pages).

What does the subscription include?

After your initial free scan, subscribers receive weekly re-scans with fresh reports delivered to their email. Each report includes a score trend graph, newly detected issues, resolved issues, and updated fix recommendations. Plans range from Starter ($14.9/5 scans) to Pro ($149/150 scans).

Can I share my audit score?

Yes! After running a free audit, you can share your SEO score badge on social media, forums, or embed it on your website. Each share includes a referral link — when someone audits their site through it, you earn free scan credits. It's our growth flywheel.

How often does the detection engine update?

Every Wednesday our engine checks Google Search Central, web.dev, Google developer blogs, and official SEO best practice sources for new rules, updated recommendations, and policy changes. The new rules are automatically integrated by Thursday.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. If the report doesn't meet your expectations, contact us within 7 days of purchase for a full refund. No questions asked.
